HI!

Tim Berners-Lee is a software engineer who invented the internet or the www (World Wide Web) in 1989 while at CERN, the europeien particle physics labrotory. He is the director of the World Wide Web Consortium that he founded and a professor in the computer science department at the the university of Oxford. In 2001 he became a fellow of the royal society and in 2004 he was knighted.

in 1994 Tim-Berners-Lee founded World Wide Web Consortium (W3c) in Boston. It was made up of companies that wanted to work together to create web standerds and also improve the web. It was quickly agreed that the technoloigy should be free of royalties and patents. Berners-Lee has served as derecter of this w3c scince it was founded. In 1995 Microsoft produced the first version of internet explorer and this comercial browser created enormus growth in websites and web users. The web was first used for e-commerce in 1997.
